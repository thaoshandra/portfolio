CASE STUDY: SERENITY TAILORING

This project involved creating and writing website content for an alterations shop, with the goal of establishing a strong online presence and providing a comprehensive reference to the shop's services. The content creation process began with research and analysis of the shop's target audience and competitors. The resulting content is engaging, informative, and tailored to the needs and interests of the target audience. The content covers the range of services offered by the shop, including descriptions of the various types of alterations and repairs, bookings, and contact information. The final website content reflects the shop's unique personality and style, while also ensuring that the key information is presented clearly and effectively. This project highlights my ability to create compelling website content that not only informs but also engages and inspires action from the target audience.

CASE STUDY: PROJECT ABCD

This project involved redesigning and optimizing the responsive design and visual interface of a non-profit organization's website (Project ABCD), with a focus on creating a individual user experience for both admins and non-admins. The design process included research and prototyping, to ensure a user-friendly and visually appealing website. The final design showcases a modern look, with an emphasis on usability. Specific attention was given to the user experience of both admins and non-admins, with features such as easy-to-use content management tools, intuitive navigation, and clear calls-to-action. The optimized website not only looks great but also provides a more efficient and effective way for the organization to communicate with its audience. This project highlights my ability to deliver a thoughtful and engaging redesign while keeping the client's objectives and the needs of both admins and non-admins in mind.
